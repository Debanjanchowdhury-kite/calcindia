🌟 Who We Are

IndiCalculator is a free, independent financial tools website built entirely for Indian users. We started with a simple belief: every Indian deserves access to accurate, easy-to-use financial calculators — without paywalls, subscriptions, or pushy sign-up forms.

Whether you're a first-time home loan applicant trying to understand your EMI, a young professional planning your SIP investments, a freelancer computing GST for a client invoice, or a salaried employee comparing old vs new tax regimes — IndiCalculator gives you instant, reliable answers.

We are an independent project run by a small team of Indian finance enthusiasts and developers. Our mission is to democratise financial literacy across India by making complex calculations simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

🎯 Mission: To provide every Indian — regardless of their financial background — with free, accurate, and beautifully simple tools that help them make informed money decisions.

💡 Vision: A financially literate India where no one overpays on loans, under-saves on investments, or misses out on tax savings simply because the right tools weren't available to them.
