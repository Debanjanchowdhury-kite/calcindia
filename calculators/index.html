IndiCalculator brings together every free financial calculator an Indian household or business needs — in one place, with no sign-up. Whether you are planning a home loan EMI, projecting a monthly SIP to reach ₹1 crore, comparing the old and new income tax regimes for FY 2025-26, working out GST on an invoice, or estimating your PPF, EPF, NPS or gratuity corpus, each tool below is built specifically for Indian rules, rates and tax slabs — and updated for 2026.

All calculators run instantly in your browser, work on mobile, and are free to use as often as you like. The loan and EMI tools cover home, car, bike and personal loans plus bank-specific SBI and HDFC home loan calculators. The investment tools cover SIP (monthly, step-up and lumpsum), PPF, FD, EPF and NPS.
