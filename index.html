About CalcIndia — India's Trusted Financial Calculator Hub

CalcIndia is India's most accurate and easy-to-use platform for financial calculations. Whether you're planning a home loan, investing in mutual funds via SIP, filing your income tax return, or simply trying to understand GST on a business invoice — our calculators give you instant, accurate, and India-specific answers.

Every calculator on CalcIndia is built specifically for Indian users. That means our income tax calculator uses the latest FY 2025-26 tax slabs from the Union Budget. Our EMI calculator uses interest rates from RBI-tracked banks like SBI, HDFC, ICICI and Axis. Our PPF calculator uses the current 7.10% interest rate set by the Ministry of Finance. We update all rates regularly so you always get accurate results.

We believe financial decisions shouldn't require a CA or financial planner for basic calculations.
